This Privacy Policy describes Our policies and procedures on the collection, use and disclosure of Your information when You use the Flamingua mobile application (the "Service").

Interpretation and Definitions

Account means a unique account created for You to access our Service or parts of our Service.

Application means the software program provided by the Company downloaded by You on any electronic device, named Flamingua.

Company (referred to as either "the Company", "We", "Us" or "Our" in this Agreement) refers to the Flamingua Team (Tobias Hagstrom).

Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

Types of Data Collected

Usage Data

Usage Data is collected automatically when using the Service. This may include your device's Internet Protocol address (e.g. IP address), browser type, browser version, the pages of our Service that You visit, the time and date of Your visit, the time spent on those pages, unique device identifiers and other diagnostic data.

Purpose of Collection: Usage Data is used to operate and improve the Service, monitor usage patterns, and diagnose technical issues. We may share aggregated, non-identifiable usage data with analytics and infrastructure providers to improve app performance.

Voice and Audio Data

The Application may collect and process voice and audio input when You actively choose to use speaking or pronunciation features within the Service. Audio recording occurs only after explicit user interaction (e.g. pressing a microphone or record button) and is not performed in the background.

Purpose of Collection: Audio data is used solely to enable speech-based learning features, such as pronunciation practice, speaking exercises, and language feedback. Audio recordings may be processed temporarily on-device or transmitted securely to third-party speech processing or evaluation services in order to provide feedback. Audio data is not used for advertising purposes.

Retention: Audio recordings are not stored permanently unless explicitly stated within the feature. In most cases, audio data is processed transiently and discarded after evaluation.

  • Apple App Store & Google Play Store: These stores process all payments. Their use of your personal information (including financial data) is governed by their respective Privacy Policies.

Data Retention

We retain Personal Data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. Usage Data is generally retained for a shorter period, except when this data is used to strengthen the security or to improve the functionality of Our Service.

Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13. If You are a parent or guardian and You are aware that Your child has provided Us with Personal Data, please contact Us.
